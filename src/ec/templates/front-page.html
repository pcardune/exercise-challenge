Welcome

The Exercise Challenge is an attempt by a few San Francisco/Silicon Valley geeks to overcome the ruins of being desk-bound.

Everyone has their own idea of success but as a general target we plan on achieving the following goals, or equivalent substitutes, within the next 12 months:

  • Run a marathon
  • do 100 pushups in a row
  • do a pull-up pyramid to 10 (that's 100 pull-ups)
  • not be afraid to take our shirts off in any San Francisco bar
  • Make other girls/boys jealous of our girl/boyfriends. (this only applies to those of us with SOs)
  • six packs, and not the alcoholic kind.

In order to coerce us into following through with the challenge, we are going to make this a very public and social affair. That means posting regular status updates on our progress including weight gain/loss, heart rate information, and of course, pictures.

This website was put in place to help us keep track of our progress. As it stands, anyone can log on with their Facebook account and keep track of their own exercises.
